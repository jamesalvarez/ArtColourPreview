Art Colour Preview

This tool allows you to drag around an image and display the colour in a bog box, which allows you to check the colour on your palette. You can increase the size of the window to get the average of the pixels within that window. For large images you can scroll the window with the image, to get to other areas!

If you are matching paint colours, remember to adjust your monitor so that your brightest colour in the photo can be achieved using your brightest white! It's normally best to do this with a pure white, but for a dark photo, you just need to make it so you can match the full range of colours using your paints.
